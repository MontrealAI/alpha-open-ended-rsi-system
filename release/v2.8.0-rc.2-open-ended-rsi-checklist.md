## Claim boundary

### Demonstrated

- Bounded accelerating-loop mechanics under deterministic local replay.
- Frozen package reuse in an adjacent control-vs-treatment mandate.
- Whitelist-bounded autonomous adjacent-domain selection with lower operator intervention.

### Simulated

- Assay outcomes and scorecard values in this demo are synthetic deterministic values.

### Not proven

- Unrestricted autonomy.
- Literal unbounded RSI.
- Completed broad real-world cybersecurity sovereign operation.
