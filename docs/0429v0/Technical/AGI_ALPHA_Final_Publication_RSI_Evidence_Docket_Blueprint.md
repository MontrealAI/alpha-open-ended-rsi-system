## Minimum claim questions

- What real task was performed?
- Which agents participated?
- Which scaffolds were active?
- Which tools were used?
- What was the baseline?
- What evidence was executed?
- What was replayed?
- What was the cost?
- What was the safety profile?
- What was the AdvantageDelta?
- What persisted into the archive?
- What did the system learn for the next cycle?

Rule: no Evidence Docket, no empirical SOTA claim.
