## What MARK does

### 1. Screens Nova‑Seeds
Every Nova‑Seed enters MARK as a possible future enterprise.
MARK asks:
- Is the seed coherent?
- Is it economically legible?
- Is it governable?
- Is it worthy of sovereign formation?

### 2. Prices and prioritizes
MARK is the layer where seeded futures are compared, ranked, and pressure-tested.

It is where the system decides which opportunities deserve:
- more attention
- more validation
- more resources
- and eventual sovereign promotion

### 3. Forms risk
MARK is not just a market.
It is also a **risk oracle**.

Its job is to make sure that a candidate future is not only attractive, but:
- reviewable
- governable
- utility-aligned
- and operationally credible

### 4. Green-flames the strongest seeds
Only a subset of Nova‑Seeds should advance.

The strongest seeds are **green-flamed**:
- they survive scrutiny
- they remain inside policy
- they become economically legible
- and they earn the right to move toward sovereign status

### 5. Promotes toward Sovereigns
A green-flamed seed does not remain a passive artifact.

It becomes the embryo of an **α‑AGI Sovereign**:
a machine-born enterprise capable of decomposing its FusionPlan into real α‑AGI Jobs and routing them into execution.
